Concierge and Traveling at Sunnyvale

There are many ways to get to Sunnyvale.

By car

The easiest way is by car, Going up 9-95 just take the exit when you see the sign for Sunnyvale road. The roaf will take you right to the resort.

By bus

Busses stop by Sunnyvale entrance. They leave from both the nearest airport and town.
